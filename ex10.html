ABOUT CHANDIGARH UNIVERSITY

"Chandigarh University is a private university located in Mohali, near Chandigarh in Punjab, India. Established in 2012 by the Punjab State Legislature, it has quickly grown into one of the leading universities in the region."

1. Academic Programs

Chandigarh University offers a diverse range of academic programs, catering to various disciplines such as engineering, business management, computer applications, law, pharmacy, biotechnology, and more.

2. Infrastructure

The university is known for its modern infrastructure and state-of-the-art facilities. Well-equipped classrooms, advanced laboratories, extensive libraries, and recreational areas contribute to a conducive learning environment.

3. International Collaborations

Chandigarh University has established collaborations and partnerships with renowned international universities and institutions. These collaborations provide students with opportunities for exchange programs, joint research initiatives, and global exposure.

4. Placements

Chandigarh University places a significant emphasis on providing students with opportunities for internships and placements. The university claims to have a robust placement record, with a high percentage of students securing jobs in reputed companies.

5. Cultural and Extracurricular Activities

The university promotes a holistic development approach, encouraging students to participate in cultural events, sports, and other extracurricular activities. This helps in shaping well-rounded individuals.

6. Accreditations and Recognitions

Chandigarh University is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and has received accreditations from various accreditation bodies. It follows quality assurance practices to maintain educational standards.

7. Entrepreneurship Development

The university supports entrepreneurship and innovation, fostering an environment where students can explore their entrepreneurial skills. Incubation centers and startup support systems are in place to encourage students to turn their ideas into viable businesses.

8. Social Initiatives

Chandigarh University is involved in various social initiatives and community development programs. This includes activities related to social responsibility, environmental sustainability, and community outreach.
